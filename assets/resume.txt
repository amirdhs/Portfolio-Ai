RESUME PLACEHOLDER

This is a placeholder for your resume PDF.

To add your actual resume:
1. Create or export your resume as a PDF file
2. Name it "resume.pdf"
3. Replace this file with your resume PDF in the assets/ folder

Your resume will then be downloadable when visitors click the "Download Resume" button on your portfolio website.

Recommended resume format:
- File format: PDF
- File size: Under 2MB
- Filename: resume.pdf

For best results, ensure your resume includes:
- Contact information
- Professional summary
- Work experience
- Education
- Skills
- Certifications
- Projects (optional)
- References (optional)
